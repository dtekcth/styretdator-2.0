Styretdator 2.0

About

This project is meant as a way of collaborating in finding the best option for the new Styretdator (section computer). The best suggestion will win a prize.

Rules

  • The competition ends at 23:59 (UTC +2) on the 18th of June 2017.
  • Only computer engineering section (Datateknologsektionen) students are eligible.
  • The winner is chosen by Emil Hemdal, the section treasurer.
  • Emil Hemdal can't compete in the competition.
  • You may provide as many entries as you wish.
  • If two or more entries by different people are identical then the first submitted one counts.
  • The competition may at any time be canceled by the host for any reason.
  • Submissions lacking the required information are disqualified.
  • The picking of the winner is final, no debate.

Picking a winner

The winner will be picked based on the following criteria:

  • Total price below 7000 SEK. (The price can change daily so make sure that you have a small buffer).
  • The total price is calculated using more respected stores prices and based on new parts.
  • The computer is within the scope of the minimum requirements.
  • The parts are compatible with each other.
The following will boost the submission:
  • The parts are available from physical stores in Gothenburg.
  • Long warranty times for the parts.

Usage

The computer will mainly be used for accounting, but occasional work with posters and other PR work will utilize Photoshop/GIMP and Illustrator/Inkscape.
The computer has two monitors.
The computer is planned to last five years.

Minimum requirements

  • 1 Displayport (on graphics card or on motherboard)
  • 1 HDMI port (on graphics card or on motherboard)

Prize

The winner of the competition will win a 250kr gift card from Inet!
