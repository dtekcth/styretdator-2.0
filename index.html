Styretdator 2.0

About

This project is meant as a way of collaborating in finding the best option for the new Styretdator (section computer) while at the same time providing a competition (with a price).

Rules

  • Only computer engineering section (Datateknologsektionen) students are eligible.
  • The winner is choosen by Emil Hemdal, the section treasurer.
  • Emil Hemdal can't compete in the competition.
  • Max price of the setup: 10000 SEK
  • The price should be based on more respected computer part stores.
  • Stores: Swedish computer part stores, preferrably physical stores in the Gothenburgh area like Webhallen and Inet.
  • You may provide as many entries as you wish.
  • If two or more entries by different people are identical then the first submitted one counts.
  • The competition may at any time be cancelled by the host.

Minimum requirements

  • 8GB RAM
  • 1 displayport (on graphics card or on motherboard)
  • 1 HDMI port (on graphics card or on motherboard)
  • Relatively quiet

Price

The winner of the competition will win the old Styretdator.
